BCBC

Birmingham Chinese Basketball Club founded and registered with BasketballEngland in 2015, was set up with the aim of support basketball development within chinese community in birmingham area. Our goal is to create a fun, positive and encouraging environment for individuals of all ages, from youngsters to seniors, as well as for teams. We strongly believe in the philosophy that "ball is life" where people learn and grow through their passion and love for the sport, which becomes a cherished treasure throughout their entire lives.

俱乐部成立与2015年, 隶属于BasketballEngland, 其使命是为伯明翰华人, 远在他乡也能持续参与自己热爱的运动.
目标是为不同年龄段, 不同水平的爱好者提供积极且有趣的打球环境.
我们始终相信‘"ball is life"的理念, 对热爱的追求和探索可以指导我们生活的各个领域, 将成为我们一生的宝贵财富.

Currently, the club is represented by a single men's team known as the Birmingham Miles, who are actively competing in the local league, WMBL Division 2.

Birmingham Miles 为俱乐部男子篮球队, 目前处于WMBL联赛第二级别.最好成绩为2017-2018赛季DIV2亚军. 每周二训练+对抗.



欢迎不同性别，年龄,竞技水平的队员组成球队.俱乐部愿意为其开发更多的比赛和训练环境.
