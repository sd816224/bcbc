Birmingham Chinese Basketball Club

founded in 2016, BCBC was set up with the aim of support basketball development within chinese community in birmingham area. We welcome people to be a part of our club, we want to represent the sport in an ethical and community driven way, promoting participation in the sport and the positive impact that competition and physical activity plays in our life, across all ages.


Miles

Currently, the club is represented by a single men's team known as the Birmingham Miles, who are actively competing in the local league, WMBL Division 2.
